Visual Styles

A brand is a company's single most important asset. A significant part of any brand is visual identity. Visual identity is the face of the brand. To be memorable and effective, your visual identity must be applied consistently across all brand touch points. This guide outlines usage requirements for every aspect of your visual identity to help you create a coherent image across all your materials.

Colors

Availity colors are fresh, bold, bright and energetic. The primary and secondary color palettes combine to lend the Availity brand a youthful, modern, and aspirational feel.

Headings

Headings are defined with the <h1> to <h6> tags.

<h1> defines the most important heading. <h6> defines the least important heading.

Search engines use the headings to index the structure and content of your web pages.

Users skim your pages by its headings. It is important to use headings to show the document structure.

Icons can be used to convey information and not simply for decoration. Icons are primarily used as a symbolic representation for the user to have a quick association between image and type.

Custom Font Icons

Using an online application, icomoon.io, we created a custom font to load icons. This creates a much smaller file that contains various images that can be used throughout the site. The icons require the use of a <span> tag to add the icon to an element.
